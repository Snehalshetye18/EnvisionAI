In response to the growing need for enhanced security and real-time monitoring, we present an innovative AI-driven crime detection system that harnesses the power of existing CCTV networks. Our system employs state-of-the-art machine learning frameworks, including cutting-edge Weakly-Supervised Learning Algorithms and SOTA Video Transformers, in combination with Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and LSTM models. This multifaceted approach enables our solution to address three critical aspects: crime prevention, crowd management, and work monitoring. To ensure immediate and efficient communication with authorities,our system incorporates a Mobile Utility. This utility serves as the bridge between the AI-driven system and human decision-makers, providing real-time alerts for potential crowd congestions and criminal incidents. Moreover, it offers authorities the ability to validate the system's alerts, thereby improving on-ground accuracy and reducing false alarms. Our solution represents a significant advancement in utilizing existing CCTV networks for comprehensive security, real-time monitoring, and data-driven decision-making.
